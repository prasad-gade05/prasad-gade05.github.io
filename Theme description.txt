DARK THEME:

Design System Specification: Midnight Neo-Brutalism
1. Design Philosophy

This theme combines the immersion of a dark-mode interface with the tactile, high-energy aesthetics of "Pop Brutalism." The visual metaphor is that of physical tokens, stickers, and playing cards resting on a deep velvet table.

Key Characteristics:

Tactile Depth: Elements do not float; they sit heavily. Depth is achieved through hard, solid-color drop shadows (no blurs).

High Contrast: Vibrant "high-voltage" accents pop aggressively against deep indigo backgrounds.

Defined Boundaries: Every element is encased in a thick, solid border.

Playful Geometry: A mix of pill shapes and soft-rounded rectangles creates a friendly yet structured interface.

2. Color Palette
2.1 Backgrounds & Surfaces

Avoid pure black for backgrounds to maintain warmth and depth.

Global Background: #12121A (Deep Void Indigo)

Card/Surface Background: #1E1E2E (Rich Navy)

Input/Inactive Background: #28283E (Muted Indigo)

2.2 Structural Colors

These define the physical edges of the UI.

Borders & Outlines: #000000 (Pure Black) - Applied to all structural edges.

Hard Shadows: #000000 (Pure Black) - Applied to all elevated elements.

2.3 Accent Colors (The "Voltage" Palette)

Used for actions, highlights, and status indicators.

Primary Action (Mint): #00FF9D

Attention/Warning (Voltage Yellow): #FFD700

Primary Brand (Electric Blue): #4A90E2

Destructive/Error (Salmon): #FF756B

Information/Secondary (Lavender): #A0A0FF

2.4 Typography Colors

Primary Text (On Dark): #FFFFFF (Pure White)

Secondary Text (On Dark): #8F90A6 (Cool Grey)

Action Text (On Accent Colors): #000000 (Pure Black - Essential for contrast on yellow/mint buttons)

3. Typography System
3.1 Font Families

Headings (Display): Bebas Neue, Anton, or Teko.

Usage: Large titles, stat numbers, button labels.

Style: Condensed, Heavy weight, often uppercase.

Body (UI Text): Fredoka, Manrope, or Nunito.

Usage: Paragraphs, input labels, list items.

Style: Rounded sans-serif, high legibility.

Data/Code: JetBrains Mono or Fira Code.

Usage: IDs, timestamps, file paths.

3.2 Hierarchy Rules

H1/Hero Titles: Uppercase, Italicized, with a text-shadow (2px 2px #000).

Button Labels: Uppercase, Bold (700 weight), letter-spacing 1px.

Body Text: Regular or Medium weight (400/500), never thin.

4. UI Physics (Borders, Shadows, & Radius)
4.1 The "Sticker" Effect (Hard Shadows)

Instead of soft, blurred shadows, use solid offset shadows to create a physical "stacked" look.

Standard Elevation: box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px 0px #000000;

High Elevation (Modals): box-shadow: 8px 8px 0px 0px #000000;

Interaction (Active/Pressed):

transform: translate(4px, 4px);

box-shadow: 0px 0px 0px 0px #000000;

Result: The element visually depresses into the screen.

4.2 Borders

Standard Width: 2px or 3px.

Style: Solid.

Color: #000000 (Black) or specific accent color for active states.

4.3 Corner Radius

Cards/Containers: 16px (Soft modern roundness).

Buttons/Tags: 999px (Full Pill) or 12px (Rounded Rect).

Inputs: 12px.

5. Component Styles
5.1 Buttons

Primary Button:

Background: Accent Color (Mint or Yellow).

Text: Black, Bold, Uppercase.

Border: 3px solid Black.

Shadow: Hard Black Shadow (4px).

Secondary Button:

Background: Transparent or Surface Color (#1E1E2E).

Text: White or Lavender.

Border: 2px solid #8F90A6.

Shadow: None (or subtle).

5.2 Cards (Containers)

Appearance: Surface Background (#1E1E2E) with 2px Black Border and 6px Black Shadow.

Header Badges: Section titles should often appear as "Stickers" that overlap the border of the card (position: absolute, top: -15px, left: 20px).

5.3 Input Fields

Default State:

Background: #12121A (Darker than card).

Border: 2px solid #383854.

Text: White.

Focus State:

Border: 3px solid Accent Color (Yellow/Blue).

Shadow: 4px 4px 0px Accent Color (Solid, no blur).

5.4 Modals / Popups

Overlay: High opacity dark overlay (80% Black).

Window: Surface Background (#1E1E2E).

Border: 3px solid Accent Color (e.g., Mint).

Shadow: Deep Hard Shadow (10px 10px 0px Black).

6. Layout & patterns
6.1 Grids

Elements should be tightly packed.

Marquee Pattern: Use scrolling text bars with solid black backgrounds and white monospace text for status updates or section dividers.

6.2 Imagery

Images should never be "naked." They must be wrapped in a container.

The Polaroid Frame: Images should have a 3px Black border.

Hover Effect: On hover, images should scale up slightly (1.05) and the border color should switch to an accent (Yellow/Mint).

6.3 Data Visualization

Charts: Use thick stroke lines (4px).

Fills: Use pattern fills (dots/stripes) instead of opacity fades.

Nodes/Points: Large, circular nodes with thick borders.

LIGHT THEME:

Design System Specification: Daylight Neo-Brutalism (Light Mode)
1. Design Philosophy

This theme translates the "Sticker Arcade" aesthetic into a high-visibility, "Pop Art" notebook style. The visual metaphor shifts from tokens on a dark table to stickers, sketches, and cutouts on a clean paper canvas.

Key Characteristics:

Paper-Like Sharpness: High contrast between black ink outlines and white surfaces.

CMYK Energy: Accent colors are saturated and reminiscent of print markers or highlighters.

Absolute Definition: No blurs, no fades. Hard lines and hard shadows define every object.

Graphic Novel Logic: The UI should feel like an interactive comic book page—flat, bold, and outlined.

2. Color Palette
2.1 Backgrounds & Surfaces

Global Background: #F4F4F0 (Off-White / Bone) - Reduces eye strain compared to pure white.

Card/Surface Background: #FFFFFF (Pure White) - Creates clear separation from the global background.

Input/Field Background: #FFFFFF (Pure White) or #EBEBEB (Light Grey).

2.2 Structural Colors

Borders & Outlines: #000000 (Pure Black) - Applied rigidly to all elements.

Hard Shadows: #000000 (Pure Black) - Essential for the "cutout" look.

2.3 Accent Colors (The "Marker" Palette)

Colors should look like fresh highlighter ink.

Primary Action (Kelly Green): #00D166

Attention/Highlight (School Bus Yellow): #FFCC00

Brand/Info (Azure Blue): #3366FF

Destructive/Alert (Hot Coral): #FF5E57

Secondary/Neutral (Lilac): #DCD6F7

2.4 Typography Colors

Primary Text: #000000 (Pure Black)

Secondary Text: #555555 (Dark Grey)

Link/Action Text: #000000 (Underlined or Bold)

3. Typography System
3.1 Font Families (Identical to Dark Mode)

Headings (Display): Bebas Neue, Anton, or Teko.

Style: Uppercase, Heavy, Condensed.

Body (UI Text): Fredoka, Manrope, or Nunito.

Style: Rounded sans-serif to keep it friendly.

Data/Code: JetBrains Mono or Fira Code.

3.2 Hierarchy Rules

H1/Hero: Huge, Black, All-Caps. Optional: White text with a thick Black stroke (-webkit-text-stroke: 2px black).

Labels: All-caps, small, bold.

Body: High contrast black text.

4. UI Physics (Borders, Shadows, & Radius)
4.1 The "Cutout" Effect (Hard Shadows)

Shadows mimic a piece of paper or cardboard cut out and placed on a desk.

Standard Shadow: box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px 0px #000000;

Hover State: box-shadow: 6px 6px 0px 0px #000000; (Element rises).

Active/Click State:

transform: translate(4px, 4px);

box-shadow: 0px 0px 0px 0px transparent;

4.2 Borders

Global Thickness: 3px solid Black.

Reasoning: In light mode, the black border is the primary separator since there is less contrast between the background colors.

4.3 Corner Radius

Cards: 16px.

Buttons: 999px (Pill) or 12px (Block).

5. Component Styles
5.1 Buttons

Primary Button:

Background: Accent Color (Yellow/Green).

Text: Black, Bold, Uppercase.

Border: 3px solid Black.

Shadow: 4px solid Black.

Secondary/Ghost Button:

Background: White.

Text: Black.

Border: 3px solid Black.

Shadow: 4px solid Black.

5.2 Cards (Containers)

Style: White Card (#FFFFFF) on Off-White Background (#F4F4F0).

Border: 3px solid Black.

Shadow: 6px solid Black.

Decorative: Use "Washi Tape" logic—small rectangular vibrant strips placed on the corners of cards for decoration.

5.3 Inputs & Forms

Field: White background, 3px Black border.

Placeholder Text: Dark Grey (#888).

Focus: Background turns slightly Yellow (#FFF9C4) or border becomes thicker (4px).

5.4 Data Visualization

Grid Lines: Dotted Black lines (1px dotted #000).

Bars/Charts: Solid Black outlines filled with "Hatched" patterns (diagonal lines) in accent colors to mimic architectural drawings.

6. Visual Patterns
6.1 Background Textures

Dot Grid: The global background (#F4F4F0) should have a 2px Grey dot grid spaced 20px apart. This reinforces the "Sketchbook/Notebook" aesthetic.

6.2 "Sticker" Badges

Status tags (e.g., "New", "Beta") should look like physical stickers.

Shape: Circular or Starburst.

Style: Solid Accent Background + White Text + Black Border.
